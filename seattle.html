project:

Information needs for small business owners in Seattle

User research
User experience design

While looking at the problem of small businesses closing down in the Seattle area, my group noticed a lack of a centralized information resource where local businesses could search for legal and practical advice on how to run their business.

After conducting further user research (through surveys and interviews) on local businesses to gain better contextual information, we decided it would be suitable to create a personalized email newsletter (primarily notifying business owners of relevant regulatory changes and how to respond) linked to a discussion forum to fulfill this need for an easily digestible information resource.
